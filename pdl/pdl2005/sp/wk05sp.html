(10/11/2005) — After spending four quarters trying to give away a victory, the Karpet Munchers got one anyway -- courtesy of the Texas Nashvillians.

Brett Favre completed 70% of his passes on his way to 215 yards in the air and 3 touchdowns. Favre has been red hot over the past four weeks with 12 touchdown passes. Pittsburgh (10/11/2005) — Mike Temme predicted a tough win and got exactly that as the Tally Wackers (2-3) squeaked by the Manic Moose (4-1) in a difficult week 5 matchup.

Although Marc Bulger has been sacked 20 times this season, he continues to find ways to produce. Bulger threw for 336 yards and 2 touchdowns. Tatum Bell broke free for 2 long touchdowns and rushed for over 100 yards for the first time this season. Bell could see more touches if he keeps this up.

Jamal Lewis finally showed signs of life this week with his first game with over 100 yards of total offense. Lewis also scored on a 15-yard reception.
