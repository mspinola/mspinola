(9/23/2003) — Reggie Wayne might not get the attention he deserves but he sure received attention from those in attendance at the stadium after scoring 29 points in leading the F-Jackie to a come from behind win over the Blitzkrieg 54 - 35.

(9/23/2003) — The Scooter's Boozers, now 2-1, toyed with their fantasy league foe, building a large lead and posting the league's best point total this week, beating Texas Nashvillians 36-16.

Tiki Barber, who touched the ball 32 times, accounted for 155 yards from scrimmage but did not score. Randy Moss battling spasms in his back throughout the game was the intended target on 10 passes, catching 4 for 88 yards including a 72-yarder.

"We were able to take the action to them and put them back on their heels," Scooter's Boozers coach Scott Arrigo said. "I don't think they were as flat as maybe surprised a little bit."

Hines Ward, who cracked the Statsworld top 5 most-started-list this week, had a 7-yard TD catch and 48 yards receiving. Ward was hit hard on his TD catch but managed to battle 3 defenders for the final 2 yards. Marshall Faulk capped off his third straight poor performance by apparently breaking his left hand. Faulk, who had 37 yards from scrimmage, will be examined when the team returns home.

"We did nothing good," said Texas Nashvillians coach Randy Folks. "We have a long way to go and a short time to get there."
