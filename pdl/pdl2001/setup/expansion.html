PDL 2001 Expansion

There will be two new teams for the PDL 2001 season. An expansion draft will be held on March 11. Each existing owner must submit to the Commissioner a list of 6 players to protect. This list is due by 2/16/01. If an owner fails to submit a protected list by February 16, then the commissioner will create one for the owner, no exceptions.

Expansion Draft

  • This draft will occur on March 11, 2001.
  • The expansion draft order will be selected by a coin flip.
  • Only the commissioner and the two expansion teams need be present, but all owners are welcomed.
  • The two expansion teams will alternate picks filling a roster from any player in the current NFL pool of players that isn't designated as protected by another owner.
  • If a non-protected player is selected from an owner's roster then the owner may protect one additional player on their roster.
  • A team may loose a maximum of 2 players during the expansion draft.
  • Any owner not attending the expansion draft must submit a list of players to protect in the event that owner has the option of protecting additional players during the expansion draft.
  • The expansion draft will last a maximum of 14 rounds.
  • Each player protected counts as one of the six protected players. That is each player counts the same.
  • After 2/16/01 the protected player list can not change, even if a fantasy trade occurs.

PDL 2001 Free Agent / Rookie Draft

  • This draft will occur in August.
  • The expansion teams will receive either the first or second pick. This draft position will be determined by a coin flip.
  • Once retainment picks are submitted (7/4/01) until after the free agent / rookie draft no pickups will be allowed. However, trades will be allowed.

PDL 2001 Season

  • One expansion team will play in the AFC East while the other will play in the NFC East.
  • The regular season and playoff schedules will remain as outlined in the rules section 2.0.
  • Keepers for next season are still due by 7/4/01, this includes the expansion teams. Any owner not submitting keepers will have keepers submitted by the commissioner. No exceptions.
