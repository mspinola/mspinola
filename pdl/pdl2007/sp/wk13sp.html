(12/4/2007) — WR Reggie Wayne scored 27 points to help the F-Jackie beat Da Funk 85-82.

Frank Gore turned in another 100-yard performance this week with 12 carries for 58 yards and 5 receptions for 53 yards. Kurt Warner brought home the win for Arizona this week, but his stats were deflated by his wide outs' injuries. Warner completed 18 of 30 passes for 169 yards and 2 short touchdowns.

"They're as good as people say they are," Da Funk coach Mark Stahlsmith said after a game that seemed to last all day. "They play hard in all phases."

Derek Anderson tossed his third 300-yard game of the season and threw 2 touchdown passes. Anderson's 304 yard performance pushed him over 3,000 yards for the season. Randy Moss bounced back from his scoreless game last week with a touchdown, but that was all the success he had. Moss finished with just 4 receptions for 34 yards.
