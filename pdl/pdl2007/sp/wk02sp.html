Pittsburgh (9/18/2007) — Quarterbacks seem to get all the credit. This game was no different as Tom Brady scored 33 points leading the Texas Nashvillians to a 105-95 victory over Manic Moose.

(9/18/2007) — The Otters followed through on its promise to throw the ball more this season as QB Peyton Manning passed for 22 points in a win over the Tally Wackers.

Peyton Manning racked up over 300 yards on 28 of 42 passing but only found the end zone once on a 22-yard pass. Warrick Dunn is in a bad spot with all of the turmoil in Atlanta. Dunn rushed for 50 yards and scored from 4-yards out. Torry Holt scored on 12-yard reception but finished with just 74 yards on 5 receptions.

"Nobody likes sitting on the sideline," Otters coach Rich Raida said. "I've coached a lot of games in this league, but this is Peyton Manning's team. I accept my role ... and I'm just happy I'm able to help him out."

Marc Bulger didn't have the most productive game, but maybe that was due to the 6 times he was sacked. Bulger connected on one score and finished with 368 yards. Deion Branch finally played like a #1 wide out for Seattle, catching 7 balls for 122 yards. Javon Walker delivered a 100-yard performance with 8 catches for 101 yards.
