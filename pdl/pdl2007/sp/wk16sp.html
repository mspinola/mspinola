Pittsburgh (12/25/2007) — After several stalled drives, the Manic Moose finally mustered enough offense to shut down the Otters 64 - 55.

LaDainian Tomlinson had take care of business as quickly as possible this week (before being rested). LT scored on a 17-yard run and rushed for over 100 yards to widen the gap in his rushing lead. Matt Hasselbeck completed 18 of 27 passes for 199 yards and 2 touchdowns. It was just the second time this season that Hasselbeck was intercepted multiple times in a game. Terrell Owens scored on a 10-yard reception and finished the game with 5 catches for 48 yards. Owens will miss the final game of the season with an ankle injury.

"Enough talk about this team being soft," said Manic Moose coach Matt Spinola. "We showed them that we can gut out a big win."

Peyton Manning completed 28 of 35 passes for 311 yards and 3 touchdowns. Manning is racked fourth in the league with 31 touchdown passes this season. Kolby Smith hasn't scored since week 12, but he's still plugging away on the yardage. Smith rushed for 60 yards and grabbed 7 receptions for 55 yards.

"Unbelievable, man," Otters coach Rich Raida said. "That's the way the game bounces."
