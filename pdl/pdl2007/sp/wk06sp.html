I think it was a great win for us and we deserved it because we played just about every phase of the game as hard as we could," Scooter's Boozers coach Scott Arrigo said. (10/16/2007) — Quarterbacks seem to get all the credit. This game was no different as Carson Palmer scored 29 points leading the Carter's Commandos to a 70-61 victory over Da Funk.

Carson Palmer is struggling to bring home wins for Cincinnati, but he isn't letting fantasy owners down. Palmer scored twice this week and threw for 320 yards. Joey Galloway turned in another fine performance this week with 4 catches for 97 yards and his second 69-yard touchdown of the season.

"After the Da Funk first score, I don't think anybody panicked," Carter's Commandos coach Chris Arenth said. "Good teams don't panic."

Chris Cooley exploded for a season high 9 catches, 105 yards and a 14-yard touchdown. Cooley was averaging 20 yards per game before Sunday's outstanding performance.
