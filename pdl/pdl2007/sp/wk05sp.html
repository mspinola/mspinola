(10/9/2007) — In a typical Sean Berecek-led performance, the Blitzkrieg overcame penalties and turnovers to out play the Da Funk 57-50.

Cedric Benson earned his yardage this week by rushing for 64 yards on 27 carries. Benson also scored his second touchdown of the season, a 10-yard run. Dallas Clark played short ball with Peyton Manning this week, catching 7 balls for 49 yards and a 10-yard score. Drew Brees is still trying to get into the top 30 QB rankings for most leagues after going scoreless for the third time this season. Brees threw for 252 yards and was picked twice.

Willis McGahee posted his fifth 100-yard game of the season by rushing for 88 yards and grabbing 7 balls for 48 yards. McGahee is second among running backs with over 600 yards of total offense.

"Too many mistakes early, and too little, too late," said Da Funk coach Mark Stahlsmith. "It was all across the board, offense, defense and special teams."

(10/9/2007) — The Dunbar Dogs beat the Tally Wackers 76-74 behind 25 points from TE Ben Watson in a hotly contested matchup that went down to the wire.

Ben Watson can't complain about all the success Randy Moss is having. Watson grabbed 6 balls for 107 yards and two touchdowns this week (and has 5 scores on the season). Plaxico Burress is doing everything in his power to be the most valuable wide out this season. Burress scored for the fifth week in a row while grabbing 5 balls for 124 yards. Antonio Gates had another typical game (for him) this week… 7 catches for 113 yards and a touchdown. Gates leads tight ends with 40 receptions for 489 yards. Tom Brady turned in his lowest QB rating of the season (105.7), which was plenty good for owners this week. Brady threw for 265 yards and found the end zone three more times. Tony Gonzalez posted his first 100 yard game of the season by grabbing 8 balls for 100 yards.

"It surely wasn't pretty. I would imagine Scott Arrigo would say the same thing," Texas Nashvillians coach Randy Folks said, referring to his counterpart. "But we overcame the adversity. In the fourth quarter, it was great leadership by Tom Brady. In that last drive he really put it together."

Braylon Edwards didn't have any big touchdowns this week, but he still gave owners 6 receptions for 110 yards.
