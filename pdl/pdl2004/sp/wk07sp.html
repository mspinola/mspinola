(10/26/2004) — Focused more on winning than avoiding mistakes , the Otters (6-1) turned in several big plays in an easy win over the Iron City Pounders (4-3).

Peyton Manning put on a clinic Sunday throwing for 368 yards and 3 touchdowns while sporting a 124.7 quarterback rating. It was Manning's fourth game this year with a quarterback rating over 100. Sebastian Janikowski got off on the right foot (his left) by kicking a 28 yard field goal. He then booted 3 field goals of 40 yards or more and ended the day going 4 for 4. Randy McMichael has been very consistent all year. He had 4 receptions for 74 yards including a 42-yard touchdown in the first half, it was his second score of the season.

"My presence is felt by everybody in the stadium," Otters coach Rich Raida said. "I couldn't have written a better script than what happened today."

Jeff Garcia threw the ball for 236 yards and a scoring strike from 21 yards out. He also gained 21 yards on the ground and scored on a 4-yard run in the fourth quarter. Larry Fitzgerald has found the end zone 2 weeks in a row. He caught 4 passes for 73 yards and a 25-yard pass for a touchdown. He has caught at least 3 passes in each game.

"I coach football, man. You want me to cry?" Iron City Pounders coach Brian McIntyre said. "I was really proud with the way that the team fought for 60 minutes."
